This password has a password strength generator and can also perform a dictionary attack upon you password. See below for further information.

 

Password Checker

Points are allocated on the following criteria

 

Length 5 Points: 1 to 4 characters in total
10 Points: 5 to 7 characters in total
25 Points: 8 or more
Letters 0 Points: No letters
10 Points: Letters are all lower case OR are all upper case
20 Points: Letters are upper case AND lower case
Numbers 0 Points: No numbers
10 Points: 1 or 2 numbers
20 Points: 3 or more numbers
Special Characters 0 Points: No special characters
10 Points: 1 special character
25 Points: More than 1 special character
Bonus 2 Points: Letters and numbers
3 Points: Letters, numbers, and characters
5 Points: Mixed case letters, numbers, and characters


Password strength is measure by the percent of the above:
>= 90: Very Secure
>= 80: Secure
>= 70: Very Strong
>= 60: Strong
>= 50: Average
>= 25: Weak
>= 0: Very Weak
